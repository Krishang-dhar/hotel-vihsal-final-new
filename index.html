DINING EXPERIENCE

Pure Veg. Pure Bliss.

Wholesome Meals, Without Onion or Garlic

Tripti Restaurant serves freshly prepared vegetarian food around the clock crafted for purity, taste, and well-being. Our Sattvic thalis, light snacks, and packed meals on request ensure your journey is nourished with the finest ingredients.

  • 100% Satvik Food (No Onion / Garlic)
  • Peaceful and Devotional Ambience
  • Room Delivery Available
  • Special Thali System for Groups
  • Hygienic, Homestyle Cooking
